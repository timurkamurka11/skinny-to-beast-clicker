# Game Design Document — Skinny to Beast Clicker

## One-line pitch

A mobile Unity clicker where the player taps to train a skinny beginner into a massive gym beast.

## Target platform

- First: Android / Google Play
- Later: iOS / App Store

## Genre

- Idle clicker
- Fitness transformation game
- Casual mobile progression

## Core fantasy

The player starts as a weak skinny guy and slowly becomes stronger, bigger, richer, and more respected in the gym.

The game should feel funny, motivating, and visually satisfying. The most important reward is visible body transformation.

## Main player actions

- Tap to perform reps
- Buy upgrades
- Unlock body stages
- Unlock better gyms
- Collect rewards
- Return daily for progress

## Core resources

### Reps
Short-term tap counter. Used for training progress and milestones.

### Strength
Main progression stat. Determines body stage and income.

### Coins
Soft currency. Used to buy upgrades.

### Pump
Optional future temporary boost resource. Can be used for short x2 tap sessions.

## Body progression

| Stage | Name | Idea |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Skinny | Very thin beginner |
| 2 | Beginner | First gym changes |
| 3 | Fit | Noticeable muscle |
| 4 | Athletic | Strong gym body |
| 5 | Big | Bodybuilder look |
| 6 | Beast | Huge exaggerated physique |
| 7 | Gym Legend | Final meme-level form |

## Upgrade categories

### Equipment
- Old dumbbells
- Normal dumbbells
- Barbell
- Bench press
- Squat rack
- Full gym setup

### Nutrition
- Cheap protein
- Chicken and rice
- Creatine
- Mass gainer
- Perfect diet plan

### Lifestyle
- Better sleep
- Coach
- Gym membership
- Recovery routine
- Discipline boost

### Automation
- Auto reps per second
- Training partner
- Personal coach
- Premium gym machine

## Monetization ideas

Implement only after the basic game is fun.

- Rewarded ad: x2 coins for 5 minutes
- Rewarded ad: free protein boost
- Rewarded ad: revive failed challenge
- In-app purchase: remove ads
- In-app purchase: starter pack
- Cosmetic skins: clothes, gym backgrounds, accessories

## MVP success criteria

The first version is successful if a player can:

1. Open the game on Android.
2. Tap and see numbers increase.
3. Buy upgrades.
4. Unlock at least three body stages.
5. Feel that the character is transforming.

## Design rule

Do not overbuild. The first game should be a clean, funny, playable tapper, not a huge RPG.
